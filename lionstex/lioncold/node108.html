savu (0725)

This procedure is called by ``newproc'' (1889, 1905), ``swtch'' (2189, 2281), ``expand'' (2284), ``trapl'' (2846) and ``xswap'' (4476,4477).

The values of r5 and r6 are stored in the array whose address is passed as a parameter.
